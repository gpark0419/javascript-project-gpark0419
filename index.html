About Me

I have lived and studied in several countries, including Korea, the Philippines, Taiwan, and the United States (Philadelphia and Illinois). My interest in technology began at Dominican International School in Taipei, where I took AP Computer Science courses (CSA and CSP) and developed skills in Java, Python, and SQL. This experience sparked my passion for solving technical problems and motivated me to expand my skill set.

Currently, I am studying at Knox College, where I am deepening my knowledge in programming languages and statistics. I have learned Java, Python, SQL, C#, C++, Haskell, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. Additionally, I am gaining practical experience as an ITS Helpdesk worker on campus.
